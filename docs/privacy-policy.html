Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2, 2026

Khanin Diagram ("the Add-on") is a Google Sheets add-on developed by Daniil Khanin and Khanin Solutions S.L. ("we", "us").

Data We Access

The Add-on reads data only from sheets you explicitly use with it (sheets named "Khanin Diagram" or created by the "Create Template" function). This data is used solely to render diagrams within the same Google Sheets document.

Data We Store

We do not store, transmit, or collect any of your spreadsheet data. All processing happens locally within your browser and Google's infrastructure. No data leaves Google Sheets.

Data We Share

We do not share any data with third parties. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services within the Add-on.

Permissions

The Add-on requests the following Google permissions:

  • View and manage spreadsheets — to read diagram data from your sheets and insert generated images.
  • Display UI — to show the diagram dialog within Google Sheets.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
